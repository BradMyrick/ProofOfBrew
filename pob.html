Proof-of-Brew Eco-Farm

Experience Sustainable Coffee Farming in Minca, Colombia

Nestled in the lush mountains of Sierra Nevada, Proof-of-Brew Eco-Farm is a pioneering sustainable coffee farm that combines traditional farming practices with cutting-edge blockchain technology. Our farm is one of the oldest and largest in Colombia, with equipment dating back to 1892 that is still in use today.

At Proof-of-Brew, we are committed to producing the finest quality coffee while maintaining a self-sustaining ecosystem. Our farm is powered by $BREW, our native cryptocurrency, which allows us to incentivize sustainable practices and support the local community.

Farm Tour Highlights

  • Educational and fun one-hour tour of the farm
  • See the historic coffee processing equipment in action
  • Learn about our sustainable farming practices
  • Enjoy all-you-can-drink coffee, brewed to perfection
  • Visit our on-site brewery for a unique coffee-infused beer experience
